811 Savings Account for Everyone

Nordic 811 is a zero balance digital bank account available to everyone (Resident Indian individuals only). Nordic 811 lets you choose between a zero balance account or an 811 Edge savings account based on your needs, which acts as a one stop solution for all your financial needs and day to-day transactions. The Nordic 811 account helps you save as well as grow your money with its varied investment offerings. The account also lets you transfer funds, pay bills and do more easily.
